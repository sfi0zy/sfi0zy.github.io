About me

Hello! I'm Ivan Bogachev. I'm a UI developer, a man experienced in modern front-end technologies with an education in computer science, psychology, music and drawing arts. I have no degrees, I'm a self-taught person. I prefer to think that I'm not a "designer that knows html", I'm a "programmer with design skills".

I'm accepting the award for my article about WebGL shaders

My hobby is music. I'm not a professional performer, but music is a big part of my life. I've been playing guitar for many years and in 2017 I wrote a book about the most popular flamenco forms. Also, I play piano and violin and compose music for a chamber orchestra. In 2018 I published two collections of my pieces for children and for music school students.

My first book about flamenco guitar

I have some mental problems. My illness called schizotypal disorder. If you don't know what is it - check out my article about it, you'll find it in my publications. I studied psychology to understand myself and to understand other people, and this year I wrote a book about human behavior. It will be available soon.
