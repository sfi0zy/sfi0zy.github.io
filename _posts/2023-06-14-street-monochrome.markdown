It's been a while, isn't it? I haven't posted anything for a few months now. I'm working on a new photography-related project. It's a lot of work. Meanwhile, I decided to share the darktable styles that I use for the street photography. Why not? My main style is the <a href='https://github.com/sfi0zy/street-monochrome'>Street Monochrome</a>. This is a high contrast black and white style, it works well in almost every situation that I encounter on the street, and now it's available as a .dtstyle file and as a LUT.
