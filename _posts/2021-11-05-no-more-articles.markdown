I didn't post anything really interesting for programmers, for a while. I feel like I should explain this fact and my thoughts about it. Most of my previous articles and examples were educational. I studied different things, technologies and tricks and taught other people to use them too. But during the past year I discovered that I already wrote about all tricks in creative front-end that are really useful in practice. I don't want to write about theoretical maybe-somewhen-in-the-future technologies, I don't want to talk about news, and I don't want to explain things like "how <i>this</i> works in JS" again and again. The really interesting tasks in my work became too big to show them in one example, or too specific to make them free from NDA. It's kind of a deadlock in this context. So I decided to take a break, maybe learn something really new to me. I don't have big plans, but photography looks interesting. I continue improving my skills and share my photos <a href='https://www.instagram.com/sfi0zy/'>to Instagram</a>. Maybe it will become a new field for tutorials someday.
