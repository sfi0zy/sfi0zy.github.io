Pyramid of adaptation (2019)

This is a book about human behavior. The book includes the model of adaptation to society, detailed descriptions of different types of behavior, and methods of influencing people.

This model had been tested, changed, and improved in various ways over years, and became the basement of the evolutionary theory. Now the pyramid is obsolete. We have a much better model. This book will be left here for history.
