Pyramid of adaptation (2019)

This is a book about human behavior. The book includes the model of adaptation to the society, a lot of detailed descriptions of different types of behavior and effective methods of influencing people. This model had been tested, changed and improved in various ways through years, and became the basement of the evolutionary theory. Now the pyramid is obsolete. We have a better model. The book will be left here for history.
